# **App Name**: Black November Land

## Core Features:

- Headline and Subtitle Display: Display a short, powerful headline focusing on the main offer and a subtitle explaining the price guarantee.
- Clear CTA Button: Present a prominent CTA button with a contrasting color and action-oriented text, such as "Quero Minha Oferta 18x" or "Falar com um Consultor."
- Price Guarantee Explanation: Show 3-step explanation of the price guarantee. 1 - Choose your equipment now in 18x, 2 - If the price drops, 3 - we guarantee the difference as a discount
- Technology Product Cards: Display product cards with a focus on the installment price, showcasing the product image, title, and the 18x installment amount.
- Lead Capture Form: Integrate a minimal form to capture leads with fields for name, email, and WhatsApp number.
- WhatsApp Integration: Integrate with Whatsapp to direct to sales team

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep vibrant blue (#0000FF) to convey trust and security related to sales.
- Background color: Light blue (#ADD8E6), providing a soft, inviting backdrop without distracting from key elements.
- Accent color: White (#FFFFFF), which can highlight essential parts and enhance clarity.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif with a modern, machined, objective, neutral look, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Mobile-first layout with clear section divisions, swipeable elements for the social proof carousel, and a fixed WhatsApp button.
- Simple, recognizable icons to illustrate the price guarantee steps, ensuring quick comprehension on mobile devices.
- Subtle hover effects on product cards and button to indicate clickability, enhancing user interactivity without being distracting.
